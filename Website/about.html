About Our Agency

Learn about our mission, vision, and the team behind Sierra Leone Football Agency

Our Story

Sierra Leone Football Agency was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to connect the incredible football talents of Sierra Leone with opportunities on the global stage.

Over the past decade, we have represented over 50 players, facilitating transfers to clubs in Europe, Asia, and across Africa.

Our Mission

To identify, develop, and promote Sierra Leonean football talent by providing professional representation and career guidance.

Our Vision

To become the leading football agency in West Africa, producing world-class players.

Our Values

Integrity

Honesty and transparency in all our dealings.

Player-Centered

Our players always come first.

National Pride

Promoting Sierra Leonean talent worldwide.

Excellence

Striving for excellence in everything we do.
